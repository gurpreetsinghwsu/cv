2. AI & ML to detect Criminal Vehicles


The use of AI and machine learning to detect criminal vehicles is crucial for enhancing public safety and law enforcement efficiency. Criminal activities often involve the use of vehicles for transportation, escape, or smuggling. By leveraging AI, authorities can automatically analyze footage from surveillance cameras, traffic systems, and license plate recognition systems to detect suspicious vehicles in real-time. This technology can flag stolen cars, match vehicles involved in crimes, or identify unusual driving patterns associated with criminal behavior. It reduces the reliance on manual monitoring, speeds up investigations, and can help prevent crimes before they escalate. With the increase in urbanization and the growing number of vehicles on the road, AI-driven vehicle detection systems are becoming indispensable for maintaining law and order.
